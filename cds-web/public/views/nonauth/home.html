MP Kesineni Nani to adopt Gollamandala village

Vijayawada MP Kesineni Srinivas (Nani) has decided to adopt Gollamandala village in Kondur mandal of Krishna district, as a part of Prime Minister Narendra Modi initiated Sansaad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) scheme.

Centre clears ORR, flyovers construction in Vijayawada

Vijayawada MP Kesineni Srinivas said that the Central government has cleared the proposal sent by the State for the construction of a flyover at Kanakadurga temple, as well as an outer ring road (ORR) for Vijayawada city.

MP Nani on Sunday informed that Union Minister for Road Transport, Highways and Shipping, Nitin Gadkari has cleared the proposal and the centre has sanctioned Rs. 19,700 crore for the ORR construction. View more ..

Vijayawada LS Segment Set to be a Role Model in 'Gram Swaraj'

For the first time in the country, Sir Dorabji Tata Trust has come forward to micro planning of an entire parliamentary constituency to place it on the path of development on the lines of ‘gram swaraj’, dream of Mahatma Gandhi.

After six months of efforts, Vijayawada MP Kesineni Srinivas (Nani) succeeded in convincing Ratan Tata to have Tata Trust take up micro planning for all the 264 villages of Vijayawada Parliamentary Constituency. View more ..
