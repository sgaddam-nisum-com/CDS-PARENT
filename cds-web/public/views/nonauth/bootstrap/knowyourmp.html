Kesineni Srinivas is an Indian business executive and a politician.[1] Since 1992, He heads the Kesineni Travels Private Limited, founded by his grand father, Kesineni Venkaiah in 1928. Kesineni is one of the largest provider of Inter-City bus transportation, serving more than 75 destinations with about 425 schedules daily across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, Maharashtra and Tamilnadu. The New Delhi based Birla Institute of Management and Technology has chosen Kesineni travels for study of its successful business model in the country. Kesineni Cargo Carriers offers value-priced overnight parcel delivery to all its destinations. The company is involved in philanthropic activities and blood donation camps. He won the Indian general election, 2014 as a Telugu Desam Party candidate. He was elected the member of parliament to the 16th Lok Sabha from Vijayawada (Lok Sabha constituency), Andhra Pradesh.

MP Kesineni Nani to adopt Gollamandala village

Vijayawada MP Kesineni Srinivas (Nani) has decided to adopt Gollamandala village in Kondur mandal of Krishna district, as a part of Prime Minister Narendra Modi initiated Sansaad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) scheme. Mr. Nani while addressing the media on Sundaysaid that he has adopted the village after following all norms laid by the Ministry of Rural Development, and as many as 19 parameters were met. He informed that the village has a total population of 3387out of which 54.7 per cent were of SC and ST population, and it is one of the major criteria for selecting the village.
